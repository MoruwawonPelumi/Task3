Here are some business strategies for this analysis
1. Revenue & Operational Efficiency
With 21,350 orders resulting in 49,574 pizzas, your Average Items Per Order is approximately 2.32.

The Insight: Customers rarely eat alone or order just one pizza. This suggests a strong family or group dining demographic.

The Opportunity: Since the average price is $16.49, look into "Bundle Deals" (e.g., 2 Pizzas + a Side for $35) to push that 2.32 average closer to 3.0.

2. Temporal Sales Strategy
The data shows classic "double-hump" demand (Lunch and Dinner peaks).

The Friday Phenomenon

Friday is your powerhouse, bringing in $136,073 (roughly 16.6% of your total annual revenue).

Action: Ensure maximum staffing and inventory prep on Friday mornings. You cannot afford "out of stock" incidents on this day.

The Seasonal Stability

July is your peak, while September/October dip.

The Insight: This follows the "Back to School" trend. In July, families are home and vacationing; in September, household budgets tighten as school starts.

Action: Launch a "School Night Special" or "Quick Tuesday Pickup" in September to bridge the autumn revenue gap.

3. Product Portfolio Analysis
Your menu consists of 32 types, but they are performing very differently.

The "Power Five" (Bestsellers)

The Classic Deluxe, BBQ Chicken, Hawaiian, Pepperoni, and Thai Chicken are your "Core" products. They are likely your most reliable drivers of repeat business.

Strategy: Don't mess with these recipes. Ensure these ingredients are always sourced from the highest-quality high-volume suppliers to maintain consistency.

The "Underperformers" (The Tail)

The Brie Carre Pizza is a significant outlier with only 490 units sold.

The Insight: High-concept pizzas (like Brie or Mediterranean) often suffer from "niche appeal" or higher ingredient spoilage costs if they don't turn over quickly.

Action: Consider a "Rotate or Retire" policy. If the Brie Carre doesn't improve after a seasonal marketing push, replace it with a new "Limited Time Offer" to keep the menu fresh.

Double Down on Lunch: Since 12 PM - 1 PM is a peak, introduce a "15-Minute Lunch Guarantee" for your Top 5 pizzas to capture more time-sensitive office workers.
